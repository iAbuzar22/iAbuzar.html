Recipe of Zarda

Ingredients

For Rice

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Food color
  • Long Lachi
  • Oil

For Prep

  • Murabby
  • Eggs
  • Kishmish
  • cham cham
  • Almonds
  • Drt cocunuts
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Milk
  • Yougurt

Instructions

Boil Rice in water with all given Ingredients with it. Flame off when 90% cooked. Strain out in a strainer. Then add Ghee into a pot. Then add sugar along with milk and yougurt. cook until thicken it up. add rice into it. vapour out extra water. keep it covered on low flame for 30 min. Dish it out and server it.
